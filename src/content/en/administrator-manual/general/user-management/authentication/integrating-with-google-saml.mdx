# Integrating with Google SAML

### Overview

QueryPie supports user integration through SAML 2.0 for Google users.
You can grant access permissions and apply policies by synchronizing users.


### SAML Configuration in Google Workspace

1. Access the Google Admin Console homepage and click Apps &gt; `Web and mobile apps` in the left menu.

<figure data-layout="center" data-align="center">
<img src="/administrator-manual/general/user-management/authentication/integrating-with-google-saml/screenshot-20240902-162650.png" alt="screenshot-20240902-162650.png" width="760" />
</figure>


2. Click the `Add App` button and then click `Add Custom SAML App`.

<figure data-layout="center" data-align="center">
<img src="/administrator-manual/general/user-management/authentication/integrating-with-google-saml/image-20240902-073821.png" alt="image-20240902-073821.png" width="760" />
</figure>


3. This is the step 1 page for entering app details.
Enter 'QueryPie-SSO' in the App Name field and click the `Continue` button.

<figure data-layout="center" data-align="center">
<img src="/administrator-manual/general/user-management/authentication/integrating-with-google-saml/screenshot-20240902-164038.png" alt="screenshot-20240902-164038.png" width="760" />
</figure>


4. On the step 2 page, enter the following values.
After entering, click the `Continue` button to proceed to step 3.

<figure data-layout="center" data-align="center">
<img src="/administrator-manual/general/user-management/authentication/integrating-with-google-saml/screenshot-20240902-164356.png" alt="screenshot-20240902-164356.png" width="760" />
</figure>

* ACS URL : `https://{QueryPie Host}/saml/sp/acs`
* Entity ID : `https://{QueryPie Host}/saml/sp/metadata`
* Start URL (Optional) : `https://{QueryPie Host}/`


5. On the step 3 page, click the `Add Mapping` button, enter all four values below, and click the `Finish` button.

<figure data-layout="center" data-align="center">
<img src="/administrator-manual/general/user-management/authentication/integrating-with-google-saml/screenshot-20240902-164739.png" alt="screenshot-20240902-164739.png" width="760" />
</figure>

* Basic Information : Primary email → email
* Basic Information : First name → firstName
* Basic Information : Last name → lastName
* Basic Information : Primary email → loginId


6. Enter your Google authentication password and click `Next` to complete creation.

<figure data-layout="center" data-align="center">
<img src="/administrator-manual/general/user-management/authentication/integrating-with-google-saml/screenshot-20240902-164955.png" alt="screenshot-20240902-164955.png" width="760" />
</figure>


7. When creation is successfully completed, the newly added app will be displayed in the list as shown below.
Click on the app to enable it.

<figure data-layout="center" data-align="center">
<img src="/administrator-manual/general/user-management/authentication/integrating-with-google-saml/screenshot-20240902-165202.png" alt="screenshot-20240902-165202.png" width="760" />
</figure>


8. In the app details, click `Download Metadata` to download the file, then click the `User Access` item.

<figure data-layout="center" data-align="center">
<img src="/administrator-manual/general/user-management/authentication/integrating-with-google-saml/screenshot-20240902-165512.png" alt="screenshot-20240902-165512.png" width="760" />
</figure>


9. Select 'Enable for all users' in Service Status and click the `Save` button.

<figure data-layout="center" data-align="center">
<img src="/administrator-manual/general/user-management/authentication/integrating-with-google-saml/screenshot-20240902-165647.png" alt="screenshot-20240902-165647.png" width="760" />
</figure>


### Configuring Authentication Method in QueryPie

1. Navigate to QueryPie Admin &gt; General &gt; User Management &gt; Authentication menu and configure as follows.

<figure data-layout="center" data-align="center">
<img src="/administrator-manual/general/user-management/authentication/integrating-with-google-saml/screenshot-20240902-170029.png" alt="screenshot-20240902-170029.png" width="760" />
</figure>

* Type : Select SAML.
* Identity Provider Metadata : Open the file (GoogleIDPMetadata.xml) downloaded from the Google Admin Console homepage, copy the content, and paste it here.
* Click the `Save Changes` button to complete the setup.


### SAML Login in QueryPie

<figure data-layout="center" data-align="center">
<img src="/administrator-manual/general/user-management/authentication/integrating-with-google-saml/image-20240723-073353.png" alt="image-20240723-073353.png" width="760" />
</figure>

Now you can authenticate with Google and log in to QueryPie through the `Login with SAML` button on the login page.
